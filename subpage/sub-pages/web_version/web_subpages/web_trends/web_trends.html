Trends Overview

Hillary
3 years ago Updated
Follow

The Trends page lets you see how your data looks over time. You can see how your calories and macronutrients have been historically broken down. You can chart all of your biometrics, and you can run a nutrition report over your recent data to see how your average nutrition looks.

The Trends Tab is separated into a number of sections:

Charts

Here you can view configurable graphs of Calories consumed, Biometrics, and Nutrients.

Nutrition Report

This page will show you average values over a given period of time for Macronutrients and Micronutrients consumed

Print Full Report

Generate a configurable full report for a given period of time, including a profile summary, Nutrition summary, records of your daily diary, and any charts you wish to include

Snapshots

Here, you can upload photos of yourself, logging your vitals at the time of upload in order to estimate body percentage and serve as motivation for your health program. (You can also upload photos within your daily diary)
