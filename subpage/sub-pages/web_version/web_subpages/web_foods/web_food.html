Foods Overview

Hillary
4 years ago Updated
Follow

Use this tab to see the nutrition breakdown of foods in our database, create custom foods and recipes, and Ask the Oracle which foods contain the nutrients you are looking for. Foods you add here via the Create Custom Food and Create Custom Recipe buttons will be saved to your list of custom foods or custom recipes. To add a custom food to your daily dairy, go to the Diary tab and choose Add Food.
