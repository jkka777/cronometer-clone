Mobile-Quick Start Guide

Hillary
6 months ago Updated
Follow

1. Download the Cronometer app from the Google Play Store or Apple Store.

2. Tap on the Sign-Up button to create a new account. Use the Login button if you already have a Cronometer account.

3. Enter your details correctly as they will be used to calculate your targets.

4. Use the Slider to choose a weight loss/gain goal. This will deduct or add calories to your energy target.

5. Enter your email address and choose a password.

6. You will receive an email to validate your account. Follow the link in the email to open the app, and you will be ready to start tracking. If you cannot tap the link in the email, copy and paste the link in your web browser.

7. Once logged in, go to the Settings tab

  • Select an appropriate Activity Level
  • Set your Macronutrient Targets
  • Fixed
  • Ratios
  • Keto Calculator - If you are following a Ketogenic diet, make sure you enter a Body Fat % to get the most accurate settings.
  • Connect your account to any activity trackers you may use. If you do so, set your Activity level none. mceclip5.png

Once you have set up your profile, you are ready to start tracking! 8. Add a food to your diary, tap the + symbol at the bottom of your screen to open the bottom console. You can also add Biometrics, Exercises, and Notes to your diary Learn more here:

  • Add a Food
  • Add an Exercise
  • Add a Note

9. After adding foods, you'll see the target bars in your Top Summary fill up. Swipe through the top summary to see your macronutrient targets and highlighted targets. Tap on any of these targets bar to access your Daily Report and see your complete nutrient breakdown for the day!

Learn how to set up your account on the Web version of Cronometer.
